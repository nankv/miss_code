ICT Captain

A big part of my life is that our school

the technology in our school.I got the
role of ICT Captain of 2018. I am REALLY

What is a ICT Captain?

An ICT Captain is someone who handles all the
technology inside the school.The Technology
Captain also educate other children about how
to use the technology, when to use it and what
to use on them. I am also the first female ICT
Captain. It is going to be very exciting!
